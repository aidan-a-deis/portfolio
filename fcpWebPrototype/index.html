All are Welcome!

We are an all-volunteer community band open to LGBTQIA+ musicians and allies. The Flower City Pride Band has been marching with pride in Rochester since 1994 and we have performed at many community events throughout western New York. There are no auditions, and we work with all skill levels. We perform great range of music from 1950's pop to today's hits so we can bring fun and enjoyment to all ages.
